University of Chicago Debriefing Statement

Study Number: IRB24-0068

Study Title: Understanding Preaching

Researcher(s): Howard Nusbaum

Sponsor: Catholic Theological Union


Purpose of the Study

This project is designed to investigate the way in which real-time preaching by Roman Catholic clergy affects people.

We aim to help those who preach to understand better what actually affects their audiences. We also hope to enable those who educate preachers to be equipped to shape their teaching more effectively.

Your responses to our survey help to inform us about various aspects of preaching.

As this study is ongoing, we ask that you not discuss this study with anyone else who is currently participating or might participate at a future time.
